SEEDING KIT
-REDBULL-

Bluetooth speaker sent out to key influencers to celebrate the branded of Tyler "Ninja" Blevins can.
Customized rugged packaging reminiscent of those found in Fortnite that showcases the new can along with
"Ninja" branded speaker cutouts and instructions.

This project started with a simple ask of building a push button playback system that would play a single song, but that wasn't enough. To make it more interactive bluetooth capabilities were added to the scope. If people were going to play there own music then the speakers needed to be able to pump out some decibels. These days what good is a Bluetooth speaker that doesn't have an internal battery so that you can take in anywhere? That is how we ended up with this final product.

After creating the laser cut files for the face plate of the speaker and the insert for top of the case it was time to get into the hardware.
