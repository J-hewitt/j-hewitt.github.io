Store Redesign
-Boston CNCPTS-

Premier Streetwear boutique in the heart of Boston, MA is now an immersive environment.
Customers can interact with an LED video screen fits perfectly into the old buildings brick alcove.
As people pick up product for a closer look the movements manipulate the video content being projected in the store.

This is a new avenue in brick and mortar stores. No longer are you just entering a store to find what you need and get out. Now the customer and the store get to play together in a space engineered to look good on social media.

The "MotherTeressa" LED video wall was painstakingly hand built to match the contours of existing features of the store. The video screen bounces back and forth between pre-recorded video content that can be easily added to via a USB interface and input from the camera mounted at the top of the arch. With several filters and effects randomly appearing over video content to help tell the story of how the customer and the store are in partnership.

That partnership is further expressed in the "Shrine" projection at the back of the store. This interactive display takes giroscopic input from the footwear on display and translates it into effects in the projection. Tilts and rolls play on each shoe effect the video differently giving you the power to VJ the store.
