Who is
Joseph Hewitt

Joseph is an inovator, designer, illustrator, developer and so much more.
Generally solving problems that others have not yet found the patience to deal with.

Story telling is at the root of everything that I do. From small creative proposals that subtly tell their narrative through obsessive detail to large initiatives that not only create a world of their own but span multiple platforms and media avenues. My story is simple, as a generally softspoken person I let my art do the talking for me.

Despite a clear drive to be an artist in some capacity since being a very young boy my training has been rather unothadox, coming from years of first hand experience and a relentless appetite to learn. Having a background in the medical field imparted me with the ability to triage a difficult situtations. Managing small teams in fabrication shops allowed for indepth understanding the importance of the process. Designing large scale structures for events made me aware of how people intertact with the spaces you provide them. Developing interactive fixtures in this digital era. These are the lessons that have shaped me into the creative force I am today, and prepaired me for whatever comes next.
