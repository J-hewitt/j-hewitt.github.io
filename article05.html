LITE-BRIGHT REMASTERED
-PWLF-

In the darkness of February in Portland,OR
the banks of the Willamette are illuminated by works of art.

The Portland Winter Light Festival is a free, outdoor, multi-day event along Portland’s southeast waterfront that features displays and environments created with light or shadow or projection. The festival is presented by the Willamette Light Brigade (the folks who light up Portland’s bridges) is hosted by OMSI and receives major support from PGE.

After hearing the theme was “The Light of Progress,” and a little reflection on what even is progress, the idea began in form. An interactive installation the Lite Brite tells the story of creative foundations, the groundwork for progress. The goal for the installation was to add a fresh taste of technology to an intuative toy that has transcended generations and has inspired imaginations. This interactive piece allows participants to exercise their own creative power, making a symbol of the future with what some might call a relic of the past.
