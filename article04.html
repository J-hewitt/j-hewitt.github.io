AR Putting Green
-PGA, DELL Matchplay-

The WGC-Dell Technologies Match Play
is a match play knockout professional golf event which takes place in Austin, Texas.

Dell Technologies partners with the Austin Country Club and the PGA to create an immersive environment of games and fun celebrating both technology and golf.

Given the opportunity to design an interactive golfing experience that each player could have a unique putting adventure. Each player would first choose a theme for their prefered course. The theme was then projected onto the putting "green" where an array of cameras and sensors would track the motion of the golf ball. Depending on its speed and location it would trigger additional visual content, and in some cases a fireworks show of cosmic proportions when a hole in one was made. After the player had played they could enter their e-mail into a capture system that gave them a digital copy of their game that could be shared via social media.
