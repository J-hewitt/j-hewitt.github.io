Colonel Sanders DJ Head
-Weiden Kennedy-

Eyes twinkle, hearts beat, and fried chicken is on everyone's mind
Never before seen, a fast food icon bridges the gap between food proprietor and DJ

Ultra, self proclaimed worlds premier electronic music festival is no stranger to over the top performances, but Colonel Sanders gave them all a run for their money in his 5 minute set.

The eyes of the DJ Head had several variations of content switching betwen pixelated visuals that synched with the beats the Colonel performed to. Giant manga eyes that twinkled, pink hearts that beat along with the base, smiley faces, scrolling colors and of course fried chicken.
