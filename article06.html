HomeGrown Athletes
-Moda Center-

Along the East Escalator of Portland NBA sports areana a story is told.
The story of the Oregon and southern Washington's community of dedication to
Education and Basketball.

The "Homegrown Athletes" exhibit collects 85 jerseys from state high schools and displays them along with sets of traditionalist drawings alongside the East Escalator, which connects the 100 level to the Fred Meyer-branded 300 level.

112 definitivly Portland icons work as visual anchors solidifying the interconnected relationship between Oregon, Southwest Washington, Fred Meyers and their dedication to the next generation. The wood work is a clear tie to the rich timber history of the region and warm neutral tones of the graphics are a rich background for the colorful team jerseys to be showcased.
